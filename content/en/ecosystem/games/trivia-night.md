# BitcoinZ Trivia Night - Fun, Knowledge, and Rewards!

Join the BitcoinZ community for an exciting monthly trivia night where knowledge meets rewards! Created by community member iheartshrooms, this engaging event brings together cryptocurrency enthusiasts for an evening of challenging questions, friendly competition, and the chance to win FREE BitcoinZ.

## Event Highlights
- **Monthly Event**: Held every 3rd Saturday of the month
- **Free Entry**: Open to all community members
- **Real Rewards**: Win BitcoinZ for correct answers
- **Diverse Topics**: Questions about crypto, blockchain, and general knowledge
- **Community Building**: Connect with fellow BitcoinZ enthusiasts

## Why Join Trivia Night?
- Test your knowledge in a fun, interactive environment
- Learn interesting facts about cryptocurrency and blockchain
- Win BitcoinZ rewards for your expertise
- Meet and network with community members
- Enjoy a friendly competitive atmosphere

## How It Works
1. Join the BitcoinZ Discord server
2. Be present on the 3rd Saturday of each month
3. Answer trivia questions in real-time
4. Earn points for correct answers
5. Win BitcoinZ rewards based on your performance

## Game Rules
- Questions are posted one at a time
- First correct answer wins
- Multiple rounds with increasing difficulty
- Special bonus rounds with higher rewards
- Fair play and respect for all participants
