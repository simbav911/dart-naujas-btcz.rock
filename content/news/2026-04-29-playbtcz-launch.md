## Two boards. Pick your style.

PlayBTCZ ships with two distinct checkers variants:

- **Spanish Mode** — the classic 8×8 board with 12 pieces per side. Tight, fast, tactical.
- **International Mode** — the bigger 10×10 board with 20 pieces per side. Deeper opening theory, longer endgames, more pressure.

Whether you're a casual player or a seasoned draughts enthusiast, there's a board for your level.

## How you actually play

Three ways to get into a game:

- **Quick Match** — get matched against a random opponent in seconds.
- **Invite a Friend** — generate a private link and challenge anyone.
- **Play vs AI** — sharpen your skills offline before going competitive.

Above the table you'll find live leaderboards, win streaks, daily quests, a friends list, and a community chat with emoji reactions. It feels like a real arcade, not a wallet UI.

## How rewards work

Win a match → earn BTCZ. The reward pool is **community-funded**, with a fully **public on-chain treasury** — every payout is verifiable on the BitcoinZ explorer. There is no rake, no house edge, no buy-in. Just the board, your opponent, and the orange.
