Play now How it works ↓

PlayBTCZ is a modern online checkers (draughts) platform where every victory stacks real BitcoinZ. No deposits, no KYC, no upfront cost — registration and play are completely free. The reward pool is funded by community donations, and the public treasury is verifiable on-chain.

Game modes

  • Spanish Mode — classic 8×8 board with 12 pieces per player.
  • International Mode — full 10×10 board with 20 pieces per player. Bigger board, deeper strategy.
  • Quick Match — get matched against a random opponent in seconds.
  • Invite a Friend — challenge anyone with a private invite link.
  • Play vs AI — sharpen your skills before going live.

How rewards work

Win a match → earn BTCZ. The platform’s reward pool is community-funded and fully transparent: the treasury address is published and you can verify every transaction on the BitcoinZ explorer. No house edge, no rake — just skill, board, and orange.

Why it’s different

  • 100% free — no entry fees, no buy-ins, no subscriptions.
  • No KYC — just create an account and play.
  • Skill-based — checkers is decided at the board, not at the cashier.
  • Multilingual — French, English, Spanish, German.
  • Live leaderboards — daily quests, win streaks, friends list, community chat.
  • Public treasury — every payout is visible on-chain.

Get started

Head over to playbtcz.xyz, pick your board, and start stacking. Players must be 18+.
